Alisa Melikova, Director

After many returns to Vyrypaev’s play "Illusions", I found the key that was essential for me: to tell a story imagined by a man in a quiet female voice — moving the main characters, two couples, into a vast, colorful, boisterous New York, to live and try to answer life’s most essential questions. As time passes, the city dissolves into the horizon; music turns into the sound of wind, voices into distant echoes, and silence takes over. Colors grow transparent. The ocean, a recurring motif, offers no peace and no answer. The film is steeped in a kind of homage to the great examples of world cinema.

Alex is a theater and film actor, recognized voice-over actor and teacher. He moved to NYC in 2014 and performed on stage and TV-series, as well as doing a lot of Russian dubbing.

Oliver is an outstanding boy and son, it’s his first acting experience.
Svetlana Kifa
Svetlana is an Honored Artist of Russia, has lived in NYC since 2003. For over thirty years Svetlana’s creative life led her to become a foremost leading lady of the stage. An author and presenter of a few cinematic programs on TV.
Olya Mikhaylova
Olya is a Russian-American actress, that performed on different stages, incl. Off-Broadway in NYC. After moving to USA in 2017 she founded a Demidov’s Actors Community in NYC.
Arnold Shvetsov
Arnold is a director, actor and teacher. 40 years of performing on lead stages in Russia, Latvia, USA. Plays and operas that he directed were performed on stages all around the world and were highly acclaimed by the press.
Pavel Shatu
Pavel is a Russian-American actor from West Siberia known for his roles in different top TV series. Pavel started his acting career on one of the KVN teams, “Region 42”. Pavel migrated to the United States in 2010.
Inna Yesilevskaya
Inna has acted on many stages in Russia and USA playing all range of the characters. Inna works as an actress at the Metropolitan Opera in NYC.
Irina Abrakham
Irina is well-recognized both as an actress and as a director. Originally from Belorussia she moved to NYC in 2007 and has been acting on stage, TV and film since then.
